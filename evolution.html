Description

Evolution created all life on Earth. Unforunately, he was black-out drunk the whole time so many of his creations are weird and/or inefficient. Given that he's had 3.5 billion years to practice, Evolution isn't really that good at his job.

Examples of Poor Design

  • Platypi have a duckbill, lay eggs, and are venomous.
  • Most of life rests on an enzyme called Rubisco due to its ability to convert Carbon Dioxide into Oxygen. Evolution, after a night of hardcore partying, decided to make this all-important enzyme incredibly inefficent and slow. When he woke up the next morning, he fixed the problem not by fixing Rubisco, but by making more Rubisco.
  • Humans grow wisdom teeth that serve no function and are often detrimental to health
  • Pandas need porn to give birth
  • Evolution gave elephants size as a defense mechanism, but if they fall, they die.

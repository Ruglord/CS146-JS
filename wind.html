Types of Wind

Small Wind
Small winds are also known as breezes. They are when clouds whisper.
Storm winds
Clouds get very emotional sometimes; their emotions are often expressed in crying that we humans percieve as rainstorms. Clouds are very vocal about their emotions, so winds are extra loud and cold during this time.

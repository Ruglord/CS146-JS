Oceans

Oceans are big bodies of salt that make a large portion of the Earth's mass.

Properties

Salinity
The Oceans are characterized by their high salinity, which is a surprise to no one because they are just large bodies of salt. As a result, oceans are the main locations of peanut plantations, as the peanuts grow up already salted. Gamers also often come here to yell at people or, as some call it, "get salty".
Depth
The oceans, in sharp contrast with their sister land structures, the salt plains, are of magnificent depth. It is hypothesized that the deepest portions of the ocean reach down to the ocean's core where the salt is melted and eventually transported back to the surface via convection currents. There, they are deposited and form blocks of salt rocks.

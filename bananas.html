Nutrition

One serving of banana contains 110 calories, 30 grams of carbohydrate and 1 gram of protein. Bananas are naturally free of fat, cholesterol, and sodium. Bananas provide a variety of vitamins and minerals: Vitamin B6 - 0.5 mg. Bananas are also extremely helpful to get Princess Peach, Toad, and Luigi to slip when driving.

Human Preference

Although the banana is one of the most popular fruits among humans, it is decreasing in popularity as time goes on. This can be attributed to the amount of effort it takes to eat it. Why take the time to peel the banana and eat it when you can just bite out of an apple? As people become lazier and lazier, bananas become less popular.

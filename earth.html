Earth

Earth is the 3rd planet in the solar system, after Mercury and Sun. Earth is named after the Greek God Earthos, the Greek God for life. He was the third son of the first god Firstgodos, whose other sons, Mercurious and Sunos, are the names of the first 2 planets of our solar system. Earth is the only planet known to harbor life.

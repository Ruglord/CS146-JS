Gag Reflex

Gag reflex is a reflex action in which the muscles of the throat contract, often so as to cause retching, typically triggered by something touching the back of the mouth

Scientific Trigger

Reflex contraction of the muscles of the throat caused especially by stimulation (as by touch) of the pharynx.

Other Causes

Other causes can be if see someone very unattractive, or your favorite team loses in overtime off of a bad call. In addition, a gag reflex can be present after a sudden realization that you havent done 3 weeks worth of homework and they're all due the next day.

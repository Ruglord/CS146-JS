Process

There are 9 simple yet extremely tedious tasks to do your laundry. Step One: Read the Label. Be sure to check the care labels on your garments and linens (Nobody does this). Step Two: Sort (Not necessary). Step Three: Choose a Detergent. Step Four: Pick a Washer Water Temperature and Cycle (Does not really matter which one you choose). Step Five: One Final Check (Nobody does this). Step Six: Load the Washer. Step Seven: Unload the Washer. Step Eight: Load the Dryer. Step 9: Hang and Fold(Optional)

Detergent
